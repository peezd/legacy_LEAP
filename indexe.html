PROGRAM LOCAL ECOLOGICAL PLAN ACTION In UKRAINE (MEP) ...

is calculated to last for three years. Seven regions were selected on a competitve basis to host a program. The purpose of the program is to work with the public, local government officials, businesses and other interested parties in the creation and execution of decisions affecting the territories. They will define the priority of local ecological problems and develop constructive decisions in regards to these problems. These priorities and actions are stated in the key LEAP ( local ecological action plan) document for each region, and together these will determine the best ideas and plans for investment.

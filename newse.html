03.04.2003
 Closing date for presentation and demonstration by public organizations for large-scale program, LEAP.
01.04.2003
 Public town meeting for ecological advice on program LEAP took place. A final list of Evpatoria region ecological problems was created in the previous meeting, and a schedule was determined for when events under LEAP would take place. Discussion on how best to attract investment to help deal with these problems was discussed in length:
  • · Condition of the drinking water, as well as the plumbing and sewer systems.
  • · Garbage collection and how garbage is disposed of.
  • · Planting of trees and shrubberies within the city.

 
21-22.03.2003

A meeting and training session took place with the purpose of working on the LEAP program. The main purpose of this training was to determine which problems facing Evpatoria would be most effective for LEAP to focus on. More than forty members of Evpatoria region organization, ""Городского Экологического Совета" (Town Ecological Advice" took part in the session. Among them were representatives of many major public and governmental organizations, as well as deputies, students, and senior citizens. An expert on ecology and public health traveled from Kiev to provide consultation during the meeting. A public city opinion poll was organized, and through this and other professional methods of information gathering it was determined that there were three major problems within the city that needed to be addressed:

  • · Condition of the drinking water, as well as the plumbing and sewer systems.
  • · Garbage collection and how garbage is disposed of.
  • · Planting of trees and shrubberies within the city.

The next stage for LEAP will be to develop an Ecological Plan Action(EPD) in order to attract investment to help deal with the selected Evpatoria region problems.
